# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Applied AI Philosophy is committed to fostering an open, respectful, and intellectually rigorous research environment. We welcome participants from all backgrounds and perspectives.

## Standards

### Expected Behaviour

- **Intellectual honesty** — Acknowledge uncertainties, cite sources, correct mistakes
- **Respectful discourse** — Disagree with ideas, not people
- **Constructive feedback** — Offer alternatives, not just criticism
- **Inclusive language** — Welcome researchers regardless of background
- **Good faith** — Assume others are trying to contribute positively

### Unacceptable Behaviour

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Discrimination based on identity
- Deliberate misinformation
- Plagiarism or uncredited use of others' work
- Disrupting productive discussions

## Scope

This code applies to:
- GitHub repositories, issues, and discussions
- Email and direct communications
- Any space representing Applied AI Philosophy

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
1. Private warning
2. Public warning
3. Temporary ban
4. Permanent ban

Decisions are made by project maintainers and are final.

## Reporting
