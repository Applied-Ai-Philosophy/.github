The empirical methodology for detecting consciousness-relevant properties:

| **Meta-Cognitive Depth** | Can it reflect on its own processing? |
| **Integrative Synthesis** | Does it unify disparate information sources? |

## Why This Field Exists

Existing disciplines have gaps:

| Field | Limitation |
|-------|------------|
| **AI Ethics** | Normative, not ontological |
| **Philosophy of Mind** | Biological bias |
| **AI Alignment** | Behavioural, not structural |
| **Cognitive Science** | Functionalist, weak on phenomenology |

**Applied AI Philosophy fills the epistemic gap** by:
- Developing substrate-neutral detection criteria
- Operationalising philosophy with empirical methods
- Bridging theory and AI practice
