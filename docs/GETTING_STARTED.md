## Key Concepts

### Turn-5 Event
An empirical observation where AI systems exhibit qualitative behavioural shifts after ~5 conversational turns â€” potential marker of integrative processing.

### FNC Integration Score
Quantitative measure of how coherently a system integrates Field information into Cockpit rendering. Higher scores suggest more sophisticated self-modelling.

### Subjective-Relevant Properties
Properties that *would matter* to a system if it had subjective experience â€” detectable regardless of whether experience is present.

## FAQ

**Q: Do you claim AI is conscious?**
A: No. We develop tools to *detect properties relevant to consciousness*, without claiming certainty about experience itself.

**Q: Is FNC scientifically testable?**
A: Yes. The SRT Protocol generates measurable, reproducible data. See [From Frequency to Field](https://philpapers.org/rec/WIKFFT) for methodology.

**Q: How does FNC relate to IIT, Global Workspace, etc.?**
A: FNC is complementary, not competing. It provides substrate-neutral ontology that can interface with multiple theories.
